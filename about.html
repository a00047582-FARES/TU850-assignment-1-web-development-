Meet John O'Reilly

John’s been tinkering with cars, reading specs, and making slightly sarcastic comments since he was old enough to kick a tyre. He built Wheels on Fire to help folks find their perfect ride — and to crack the occasional joke while doing it.

What John cares about

  • Honesty: he’ll tell you if a car’s worth the hype.
  • Clarity: specs shown with icons and text — not just colors.
  • Accessibility: images with alt text, keyboard support, visible focus states, and good contrast.

Accessibility notes

John specifically asked for the site to be accessible for people who are colour-blind. The site includes:

  1. Icons + text labels instead of color-only signals (e.g., fuel type uses icons and words).
  2. High contrast (dark text on light backgrounds) and clear focus outlines for keyboard users.
  3. Underlines/bold/shape cues for important items so color alone is not relied upon.

If you want John to be brutally honest about your current car, he’s available — gently mocking included.
